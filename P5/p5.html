Assignment #6 Data Portrait

In this assignment, we need to collect data around us to convert it into images. What I chose was how many times I clicked the skip button on the music app within a week. If I clicked the space bar first, it would play the song I listened to the most times last year. The skipping criteria for clicking on a character is to switch between different backgrounds to express the location where I often listen to songs, while the large and small dots express the mood when clicking for different purposes.

Assignment #9 Experimental Camera

In this assignment, I referred to facial filters commonly used in daily life and combined mosaic effects with random sorting effects. I captured several parts of the user's face in my work and pieced them together. I also added some other photos to make the entire filter more aesthetically pleasing. When the mouse hovers over each mosaic at the same time, these images will be pushed twice, adding some interactivity to the entire camera.
